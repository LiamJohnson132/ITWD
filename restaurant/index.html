Restaurant Description

Boat Brothers, known locally as Boat Bros, is a simple family-style restaurant. Our goal is to serve the proud citizens of Karrdowan and provide a "home away from home" for all those buy nights where cooking at home is too tedious. Or perhaps you're on a date night with that new girl you met. Regardless, our goal is to serve, and service is our specialty.
